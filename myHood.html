Lehi, UT near "Silicon Slopes"

Lehi is a small town just south of Salt Lake City, UT. It's referred to as "Silicon Slopes" because of the density of tech companies that call this region home.

The following will include a few things to do in town and some of the best features to check out.

Restaurants

Here are a few of my favorite places:

  • Mo'Bettas
  • The Original Pancake House
  • Teriyaki Grill

Things to do

Here are a few things to do:

  • Snowboarding and hiking at Sundance
  • Fishing and boating on Utah Lake
  • Dining and hiking in Park City

Weather

Here are some details around each season:

  • Winter is cold with lots of snow activities to enjoy
  • Spring still has snow activities, but the valley is much more pleasant and green.
  • Summer is hot, but nights are great. Canyons give a pleasant retreat from the heat.
  • Fall is exciting because all the colors start to change and temperatures start to come down.
